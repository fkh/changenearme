Change Near Me

Discover community projects around you! Use changenear.me on your mobile browser to find projects from ioby and Change By Us. Your phone should find you and zoom the map to your current location. Click a map pin to find out more about any project.

changenear.me usesScraperwiki to collect info on projects from ioby and Change By Us. Find it on github. Wouldn't it be cool if you could get all your planning info like this? fkh cobbled this together as a Friday project at OpenPlans.
